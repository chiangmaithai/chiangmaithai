About Us

Our Thai chefs have an abundance of experience in Thai cuisine and bring a quality of food to downtown Clearwater that equals that of any first class restaurant in Thailand.

Chiang Mai Thai and Sushi Bar fuses authentic Thai ambience with full natural flavors.  It's soft subtle colors and peaceful elegant atmosphere create a romantic setting.  We pride ourselves on serving only the finest and freshest ingredients cooked to a high standard.  The food is authentic, the ambience friendly, and the staff offer you the traditional warmth of Thai hospitality.  We hope to have the pleasure of your company at Chiang Mai Thai and Sushi Bar.
