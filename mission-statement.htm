Chiang Mai Thai & Sushi Bar offers bold and authentic flavors of Thai and Sushi located in beautiful downtown Clearwater.  Our talented, experienced chefs create authentic Thai and Sushi entrees using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.  Exotic sauces and flavors intermingle on your plate creating an exciting taste journey you can experience only in our restaurant.

Vision Statement

To provide our customers with a realistic Thai dining experience, which is enjoyable, delicious, and represents the best quality of service.

Our customers should experience Thai culture, and Thai dishes which are the same as those found if they actually traveled to Thailand for their meal.  Our waiters and waitresses will dress in classic Thai apparel, and their demeanor will be both polite and efficient.  Our ambience reflects classic Thai decor, and traditional music.  The 'wai' will be used as a gesture of respect for our customers, the same as they would see in Thailand.

Chiang Mai should be known as the 'High Class restaurant at middle-class prices'.
